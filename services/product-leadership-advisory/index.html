Frequently asked questions

What is Product Leadership Advisory?

It is senior-level guidance on AI product strategy, portfolio choices, organisation design and execution—without replacing your internal leadership.

Who should use this advisory service?

Founders, CXOs and product heads who need an experienced sparring partner on AI direction, not generic coaching.

Can you advise founders on AI product strategy?

Yes. Sessions focus on use-case focus, roadmap credibility, risk framing and narratives leadership can defend.

Can you help product managers move into AI product leadership?

Yes. Advisory covers portfolio thinking, stakeholder management, governance and how to lead non-deterministic product systems.

Do you help with roadmap and portfolio decisions?

Yes. Reviews cover prioritisation logic, dependency risk, resourcing and what to stop—not only what to start.

Can you support executive presentations and leadership narratives?

Yes. Support includes storyline structure, metrics that matter and honest trade-off framing for boards and CXOs.
