Frequently asked questions

How can AI improve healthcare and insurance workflows?

By automating document intake, extraction and routing; supporting rule evaluation; and giving operators decision assistance—with humans retaining accountability for exceptions.

Can AI help in claims and pre-authorisation automation?

Yes. High-volume, repeatable steps are strong candidates when paired with audit trails, confidence scores and escalation to specialists.

What is document intelligence in healthcare operations?

It is the product layer that classifies, extracts and validates clinical and billing documents so operators spend time on judgment—not re-keying.

How do you maintain auditability in AI-driven workflows?

Through logged inputs, model outputs, rule decisions, human overrides and traceable sources—designed as product requirements, not afterthoughts.

Can AI replace human processors or doctors?

No. AI should augment processors and clinicians—handling volume and consistency while humans own exceptions, liability and clinical judgment.

What kind of outcomes can healthcare AI create?

Typical targets include lower TAT, higher productivity, fewer rework loops and better consistency—measured per workflow stage.
