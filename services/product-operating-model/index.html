Quick answer

A Product Operating Model defines how product teams discover, prioritise, build, govern and measure product work. Samadhan Mishra helps companies create operating models for AI-native products, including governance, roadmap rituals, templates, metrics and execution cadence.

Frequently asked questions

What is a product operating model?

It is how a team discovers, prioritises, builds, governs and measures product work—roles, rituals, templates and decision rights included.

Why do AI teams need a different operating model?

AI adds evals, model memos, cost controls and non-deterministic release criteria—requiring governance that does not block learning.

Can you help create PRD and roadmap templates?

Yes. Templates cover workflow context, success metrics, eval bars, HITL policy and rollout gates—not only feature lists.

How do you improve product-engineering alignment?

Through shared intake, definition-of-ready, review cadence and metrics both sides trust—reducing rework and ambiguity.

Is this useful for startups or only enterprises?

Both. Startups gain discipline before chaos; enterprises regain focus when AI pilots multiply without portfolio view.

Can this help with executive governance?

Yes. Operating models include executive review rhythms, portfolio metrics and clear escalation paths.
