About this book

AI Learning is a structured book-style learning path for understanding advanced AI concepts in a practical, product-friendly way — an AI Product Manager learning path from LLM mechanics to enterprise AI transformation. Content aligns with the AI Product Leader syllabus; modules build sequentially.
