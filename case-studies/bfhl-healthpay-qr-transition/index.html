HealthPay-BajajPay QR Transition & Payment Migration

Case study: Migrating provider QR payment infrastructure to BajajPay, defining integration and onboarding designs for secure merchant onboarding.

Context

BFHL HealthPay 2023. Transition and integration design for migrating payment QR infrastructure to BajajPay.

Business problem

Merchant/provider payments needed to migrate to the BajajPay QR network. The transition affected provider onboarding, QR mapping, logistics, settlements, refunds, and communications.

User or operational pain

A direct cutover risked disrupting provider operations. Operations and logistics teams needed clear processes for QR softcopy generation, logistics tracking, and support during the transition.

Product intervention

Designed a controlled migration roadmap: defined integration endpoints for QR generation, transaction/settlement feeds, refunds, support ops, and provider communication flows.

AI capability used

Auto-generation algorithms for QR codes, automated transactional notifications, and API-led integration flows.

Samadhan's role

Samadhan defined product requirements, solutioning, integration coordinates, QR migration planning, logistics SOP, and UAT support.

Business outcome

Successfully completed integration design and UAT. Delivered the codebase to production, establishing the foundation for smooth merchant onboarding and operations.

Lessons for other companies

Migration is a logistics and communication challenge, not just a tech stack challenge. Define clear UAT parameters and operational SOPs early.
