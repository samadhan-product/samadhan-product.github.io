Frequently asked questions

Who is Samadhan Mishra?

Samadhan Mishra is an AI Product Leader and Product Management Consultant with 13+ years across HealthTech, insurance TPA, EdTech, FinTech, and SaaS. He helps companies design and ship AI-native products, agentic workflows, healthcare automation, and scalable product operating models.

What does an AI Product Leader do?

An AI Product Leader sets product direction for AI features and platforms: use-case selection, workflow design, model and architecture choices, quality and safety bars, unit economics, and cross-functional execution from prototype to production.

What is the difference between an AI Product Manager and a traditional Product Manager?

An AI Product Manager owns non-deterministic systems: eval design, prompt and context strategy, model tiering, latency and cost tradeoffs, grounding and guardrails, and human-in-the-loop design. Traditional PMs focus more on deterministic software delivery.

How can an AI Product Management Consultant help startups and enterprises?

A consultant accelerates decisions that are expensive to learn by trial: which workflows to automate, how to structure pilots, what to measure, how to route models by task, and how to align product, engineering, and operations on a production path.
