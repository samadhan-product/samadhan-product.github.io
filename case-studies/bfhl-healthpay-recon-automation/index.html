HealthPay Recon & Settlement Automation

Case study: Automating multi-ledger pay-in and pay-out reconciliation for doctor and lab payouts, enabling T+1 payments via RazorpayX.

Context

BFHL HealthPay 2023. Executed pay-in and pay-out automation across customer QR payments, vouchers, PG settlements, and doctor/lab payouts.

Business problem

HealthPay transactions were complex multi-ledger compositions (customer payments, Voucherify, Healthcoin, and Claims benefits). Reconciling these manually against PG settlement and bank statements was slow and error-prone.

User or operational pain

Payout delays led to doctor and lab partner escalations. Manual correction of raw transaction data lacked audit trails and maker-checker validation, creating financial risk.

Product intervention

Created an API-led reconciliation engine (PayRx) that ingested gateway settlements, verified gross transaction values, ran payout logic, and triggered payouts via RazorpayX.

AI capability used

Multi-ledger matching engine algorithms matching transaction values against PG settlement files and bank feeds, automated payout triggering, and anomaly highlighting.

Samadhan's role

Samadhan was Lead PM for the entire project: owned the PRD, user stories, matching engine specifications, audit control design, and participated in daily operations review until stabilization.

Business outcome

Eliminated manual reconciliation effort completely. Payouts moved to T+1, payout failures and escalations reduced significantly, and auditability improved via maker-checker approvals.

Lessons for other companies

Financial automation requires strong fallback designs. Retain manual upload tools and define clear business continuity plans for system downtime.
