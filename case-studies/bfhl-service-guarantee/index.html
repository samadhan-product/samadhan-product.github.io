BFHL Service Guarantee — Lab & Doctor Booking Fulfilment Assurance

Case study: Designing an SFDC-led fulfilment assurance operating model to guarantee lab test and doctor appointment execution, reducing customer complaints.

Context

Bajaj Finserv Health Limited (BFHL) 2022. The project covered fulfilment assurance for lab test bookings and doctor appointments across SFDC, HRx, and consumer-facing channels.

Business problem

Customers could book services digitally, but booking did not guarantee provider confirmation or actual fulfilment. Manual coordination across multiple systems resulted in high cancellation and silent booking failures.

User or operational pain

Operations teams lacked visibility into SLA breaches. Customers suffered from broken journeys and high anxiety after booking, leading to volume escalations and complaints.

Product intervention

Product-led shift from booking to fulfilment assurance: created an SFDC case-management flow integrated with HRx, app-facing status tracking, automated WhatsApp/SMS notifications, and voice-blast escalations.

AI capability used

Automated status synchronization workflows, TAT-based escalation alerts, and automated reminder logic triggering communication rules.

Samadhan's role

Samadhan owned the complete product solutioning as Lead PM: defined SFDC case states, synced HRx integration requirements, designed provider response states, and coordinated go-live of the complete flow.

Business outcome

The complete Service Guarantee flow went live. Fulfilment rate was established as the primary business metric, resulting in a significant reduction in customer complaints and improved provider accountability.

Lessons for other companies

Reframer from booking management to fulfilment assurance. The product is not successful when a booking is created, but when the service is actually completed.
