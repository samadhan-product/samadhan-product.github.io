AI-Powered Cognitive Decision Engine for Healthcare Operations

Case study: an AI-led decision intelligence platform for medical and billing document understanding, rule evaluation, and human-in-the-loop operator support.

Context

Vidal Health TPA modernization under Bajaj Finserv Health. The project covered the Enigma Cognitive Engine/Document Management System (DMS) and Copilot layer across IPD Cashless operations.

Business problem

Processors spent a large portion of their AHT reading unstructured medical bills, discharge summaries, and pre-auth forms, manually mapping packages, and applying room category caps.

User or operational pain

Inconsistent field extraction quality and wrong package matching created high audit errors. Operators lacked confidence in automated recommendations, leading to high override rates.

Product intervention

Created a cognitive automation layer (Enigma/DMS/Copilot) featuring automated OCR extraction, structured data proofing, confidence scoring, and an interactive decision support UI.

AI capability used

Computer vision and NLP for medical document classification, structured field extraction, automated Room Category and Tariff validation, and issue highlighting with audit-trail preservation.

Samadhan's role

Samadhan led product strategy for the AI-powered decision support platform: designed operator journeys, confidence-band logic, exception routing pathways, and maker-checker approval controls.

Business outcome

Reduced manual handling of unstructured document types, automated room rent and tariff limit compliance checks, and built a fully auditable decision-support trail.

Lessons for other companies

Operator trust is a product requirement. Build trust by displaying extraction confidence, linking directly to source documents, and designing overrides as first-class controls.
