AI Learning Platform for Schools — Structured AI Education

Case study: B2B EdTech platform design for structured AI curriculum, teacher enablement, and practical adoption in schools.

Context

Schools need practical AI literacy programs—not generic tool training—with curriculum alignment, teacher support, and measurable learner progress.

Business problem

Ad-hoc AI workshops did not scale. Institutions lacked LMS-aligned journeys, projects, and certification paths.

User or operational pain

Administrators could not justify spend without outcomes; teachers needed ready-to-run modules and assessment rubrics.

Product intervention

An AI education platform concept: structured curriculum, project-based learning, teacher playbooks, LMS integration, and certification.

AI capability used

Guided AI projects, safe tooling boundaries, prompt and workflow templates, and analytics on learner engagement—not unbounded chat in classrooms.

Samadhan's role

Samadhan led product definition, partner segmentation, packaging, and go-to-market narrative for institutional buyers.

Business outcome

Clear product architecture for pilots with schools, defined delivery model, and differentiation from consumer AI apps.

Lessons for other companies

Education AI products need governance, pedagogy, and procurement clarity—not feature parity with ChatGPT.
