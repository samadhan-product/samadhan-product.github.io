OPD Reimbursement Claims Processing Automation

Case study: Revamping OPD reimbursement claims processing on Salesforce to increase processor capacity by 30% through automated rule engine and Customer 360 views.

Context

Bajaj Finserv Health Limited (BFHL) 2022 OPD Reimbursement claims-processing project executed on Salesforce.

Business problem

Processors manually pulled claims (Service Requests) from lists, switched between multiple screens for client and policy history, and ran benefit checks manually using external notes.

User or operational pain

Processor capacity was capped at 75 claims/day. Duplicate claims created by customers sending requests via different modes went undetected, leading to high operational leakages.

Product intervention

Product discovery and solutioning: built a Customer 360 console on Salesforce, round-robin auto-allocation, configurable rule engine for eligibility and duplicate checks, and embedded document management.

AI capability used

Automated duplicate detection logic, rule-engine rules for eligibility checks, and OCR document processing validation.

Samadhan's role

Samadhan led product requirement specifications and discovery: sat with processors at the call center to observe workflows, mapped leakage areas, and designed the Salesforce capability tracks.

Business outcome

Processor productivity increased from 75 to 130 claims/day (30% efficiency improvement achieved). STP pass rate reached 50% and decision accuracy stabilized at 99%.

Lessons for other companies

Discovery must happen on the operating floor. True efficiency comes from screen-consolidation and workflow automation rather than standalone features.
